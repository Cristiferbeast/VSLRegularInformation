Speed and Timing Stats

Speed Stats

Speeds of Characters has been measured in the past by VSL RI, Which found the following data regarding them

  • 4 m/s unencumbered walk speed
  • 5 m/s sprint speed
  • 30 seconds max time Sprinting Unencumbered without Caffeine
  • 8 sec recovery from no stamina to max
  • Crouch Walking moves at 2.5m/s
  • Male and Female Outlanders Move at the Same Speed

Further Math has been done to understand these stats better,
Players Move at 4.7m/s or 282m/minute or 16.92km/h
